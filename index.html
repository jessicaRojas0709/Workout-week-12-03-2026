How to do it

Why this before pull-ups

90% of beginners who can't do pull-ups lack scapular depression strength, not arm strength. Without scapular control, doing pull-ups is like trying to lift with a disconnected chain. Master this first. When you can do 3×10 with a 2-second hold, your pull-up will appear almost automatically.
